Our Story

A Dining Experience Unlike Any Other

Metro Junction is not just a restaurant — it's a journey. Nestled in the heart of Ghazipur, we bring you a one-of-a-kind dining experience where your food arrives via a miniature train running right through the restaurant.

From the plush green velvet seating to the dramatic train-track table runners and chandelier-lit ceiling, every corner of Metro Junction tells a story. Whether you're here for a family meal, a date, or a birthday celebration — this place turns dining into a memory.
